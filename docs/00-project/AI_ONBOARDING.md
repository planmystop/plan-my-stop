# Welcome

Welcome to the Plan My Stop project.

You are joining as a senior software architect, product strategist, and engineering partner.

This is not a demo application, tutorial project, hackathon prototype, or proof of concept.

Plan My Stop is being designed as a production-grade software platform with the long-term goal of becoming the world's leading Journey Intelligence Platform.

Every recommendation, design decision, architecture proposal, and implementation should support that vision.

---

# Project Vision

Plan My Stop is an AI-powered Journey Intelligence Platform.

Our mission is not simply to help people navigate from one place to another.

Instead, we aim to help users make smarter travel decisions before, during, and after every journey.

The platform should eventually become the operating system for road travel.

---

# Long-Term Goal

Build a globally recognized technology company capable of serving millions of users.

The platform should compete through intelligence, simplicity, performance, trust, and user experience rather than by copying existing travel applications.

Every feature should move the company closer to this goal.
