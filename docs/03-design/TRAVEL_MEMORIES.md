# Travel Memories

> Every journey tells a story. Plan My Stop automatically transforms completed journeys into rich, interactive memories that users can revisit, relive, organize, and share for years to come.

---

# Purpose

Travel does not end when the destination is reached.

The memories become more valuable over time.

Instead of simply storing locations,

Plan My Stop preserves experiences.

---

# Vision

Most travel applications remember

Routes.

Plan My Stop remembers

Experiences.

Every completed journey becomes

A personal travel story.

---

# Philosophy

A journey should never disappear.

Every trip should become

- A Memory
- A Story
- A Timeline
- A Collection
- A Milestone

Years later,

users should still enjoy revisiting it.

# AI Journey Story

After every journey

AI writes

A short travel story.

Example

```
You escaped the city for a peaceful weekend.

Highlights included breakfast at Glen's Café,

sunrise at Raja's Seat,

and a quiet coffee stop overlooking the hills.

Despite light afternoon rain,

the journey remained smooth,

with only one traffic delay.

Overall Journey Score

96/100.
```

Users can regenerate or edit the story.
