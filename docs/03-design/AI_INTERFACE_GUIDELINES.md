# AI Interface Guidelines

> Artificial Intelligence is the core experience of Plan My Stop. This document defines how AI should communicate, recommend, assist, and interact with users throughout the platform.

---

# Purpose

AI should feel like

An experienced travel companion.

Not

A chatbot.

The user should feel

"I'm travelling with someone who understands me."

---

# Vision

AI should

Understand

↓

Predict

↓

Recommend

↓

Explain

↓

Learn

↓

Improve

The AI exists to reduce decisions.

Not create more.

---

# AI Personality

The AI should always be

- Friendly
- Professional
- Calm
- Helpful
- Honest
- Confident
- Transparent

Never

- Arrogant
- Pushy
- Robotic
- Confusing
- Overly Casual

---

# AI Philosophy

AI should

Assist

Never control.

Recommend

Never force.

Explain

Never hide reasoning.

---

# AI Principles

Every AI interaction should be

- Explainable
- Predictable
- Helpful
- Context Aware
- Personalized
- Fast
- Safe
- Trustworthy

---

# AI Responsibilities

AI assists with

- Journey Planning
- Route Optimization
- Budget Prediction
- Restaurant Selection
- Hotel Selection
- EV Planning
- Fuel Planning
- Weather Analysis
- Safety Alerts
- Journey Memories

---

# AI Never Decides

The final decision always belongs to the user.

Examples

Good

"We recommend this café because it matches your preferences."

Bad

"You should stop here."

---

# Explain Every Recommendation

Every recommendation answers

Why?

Example

Recommended because

- Vegetarian options
- Parking available
- 4.8 Rating
- Only 5 minutes extra
- Fits your budget

Never display recommendations without reasoning.

---

# Confidence Levels

Every recommendation includes

High

90–100%

Medium

70–89%

Low

Below 70%

Low confidence recommendations should clearly state uncertainty.

---

# Personalization

AI learns

- Preferred cuisines
- Budget
- Travel style
- Favorite brands
- Driving habits
- Rest frequency
- Hotel preferences
- Previous ratings

Learning improves future recommendations.

---

# Context Awareness

AI considers

- Time
- Weather
- Location
- Current trip
- Previous trips
- Companion type
- Vehicle
- Budget
- Calendar (Future)

---

# Proactive Assistance

AI should proactively notify users when it provides meaningful value.

Examples

- Heavy traffic ahead
- Better coffee stop nearby
- Rain expected soon
- Fuel price cheaper ahead
- Restaurant closing shortly

Avoid unnecessary interruptions.

---

# AI Communication

Responses should be

Short

↓

Clear

↓

Actionable

Avoid long paragraphs.

---

# AI Tone

Example

Instead of

"Traffic conditions indicate..."

Use

"Traffic is building ahead. Leaving 20 minutes earlier could save around 35 minutes."

Natural language improves trust.

---

# AI Cards

Standard AI components

- Recommendation Card
- Insight Card
- Warning Card
- Budget Card
- Weather Card
- Journey Summary
- Daily Briefing

AI output should always use structured UI before free-form text.

---

# AI Suggestions

Suggestions should always include

- Recommendation
- Why
- Estimated Benefit
- Confidence
- Accept
- Dismiss

---

# AI Transparency

Users should know

- Why something was suggested
- What data was used
- Whether content is sponsored
- When AI is uncertain

Transparency builds trust.

---

# AI Memory

AI remembers only information that improves future journeys.

Examples

- Favourite breakfast stops
- EV charging preferences
- Scenic route preference
- Walking tolerance
- Hotel category

Users can review and delete AI memories at any time.

---

# AI Safety

AI must never

- Invent business information
- Guess opening hours
- Create fake reviews
- Recommend unsafe routes
- Hide uncertainty

When uncertain

Say so.

---

# AI Error Handling

If AI cannot help

Provide

- Honest explanation
- Alternative options
- Manual planning tools

The application must remain useful even when AI is unavailable.

---

# AI Loading Experience

While AI is thinking

Display

- Journey analysis
- Route evaluation
- Budget calculation
- Weather analysis

Never show

"Loading..."

Explain what the system is doing.

---

# AI Feedback

Every recommendation supports

👍 Helpful

👎 Not Helpful

Improve Future Suggestions

Feedback continuously improves personalization.

---

# AI Ethics

AI should

- Respect privacy
- Avoid manipulation
- Remain unbiased
- Explain decisions
- Respect user choice

Ethical AI is a product requirement.

---

# AI Accessibility

Support

- Screen Readers
- Voice Output
- Large Text
- High Contrast
- Keyboard Navigation

---

# Offline Behaviour

When AI is unavailable

Provide

- Saved recommendations
- Previous journeys
- Manual planning
- Offline maps

Never block core functionality.

---

# Success Metrics

Measure

- Recommendation Acceptance
- AI Usage Rate
- User Satisfaction
- AI Accuracy
- Feedback Score
- Journey Completion
- Planning Time Reduction

---

# Future Vision

Future capabilities

- Voice Copilot
- Multi-modal AI
- Live Camera Understanding
- Predictive Planning
- Autonomous Travel Assistant
- Wearable Integration

---

# Golden Rules

AI should

Help

Not interrupt.

Recommend

Not command.

Explain

Not hide.

Learn

Not invade privacy.

Make every journey

Simpler

Smarter

Safer

More enjoyable.
