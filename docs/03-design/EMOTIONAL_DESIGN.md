# Emotional Design

> Great products do more than solve problems. They create emotions. Plan My Stop should reduce stress, increase confidence, create excitement, and transform every journey into a memorable experience.

---

# Purpose

People rarely remember

Buttons.

Menus.

Colors.

They remember

How the product made them feel.

Our goal is to create positive emotions before, during and after every journey.

---

# Vision

Every interaction should leave the user feeling

Safe

↓

Confident

↓

Excited

↓

Relaxed

↓

Inspired

Travel should feel enjoyable before the journey even begins.

Easy

Not complicated.

Hopeful

Not stressful.

Exciting

---

# Departure Experience

Before departure

The application should reassure users.

Example

```
Everything is ready.

Weather looks great.

Traffic is light.

Enjoy your journey.
```

---

# During Travel

The AI should reduce stress.

Examples

```
No worries.

Traffic is slightly heavier.

A better route saves
18 minutes.
```

---

# Unexpected Problems

When something goes wrong

Remain calm.

Example

```
Heavy rain has started.

I've found a safer route.

Arrival will be delayed
by only 12 minutes.
```

Never create panic.
