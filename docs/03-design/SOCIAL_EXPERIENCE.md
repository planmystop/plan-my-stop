# Social Experience

> The Social Experience enables travelers to safely share journeys, collaborate with friends and family, discover community experiences, and inspire others. It focuses on meaningful travel connections rather than traditional social networking.

---

# Purpose

Travel is often better when shared.

Plan My Stop should help users

- Plan together
- Travel together
- Remember together
- Inspire others

without becoming another social media platform.

---

# Vision

People should share

Experiences.

Not attention.

The platform celebrates journeys,

not popularity.

---

# Philosophy

Explore

↓

Share

↓

Inspire

↓

Connect

↓

Remember

↓

Explore Again
