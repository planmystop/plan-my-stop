# Notification Experience

> Notifications are an extension of the AI travel companion. Every notification should provide timely, relevant, and actionable information that improves the user's journey without creating distraction or fatigue.

---

# Purpose

Notifications exist to help users.

Not to increase engagement metrics.

Every notification should answer

"Why is this useful right now?"

If it cannot answer that,

it should not exist.

---

# Vision

Users should feel

"The app reminded me exactly when I needed it."

Never

"This app keeps disturbing me."

---

# Philosophy

Detect

↓

Understand

↓

Prioritize

↓

Notify

↓

Help

↓

Disappear

Notifications should solve problems.

# Driving Mode

During driving

Allow

- Navigation
- Safety
- Emergency
- Critical Traffic

Delay

- Memories
- Promotions
- Achievements

Driving safety always comes first.

# Golden Rules

Never interrupt without value.

Never notify without purpose.

Never create anxiety.

Always explain.

Always respect the user's attention.

Every notification should make the journey safer, smarter, or more enjoyable.
