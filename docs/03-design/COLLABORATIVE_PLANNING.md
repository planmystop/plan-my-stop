# Collaborative Planning

> Collaborative Planning allows multiple people to plan, discuss, customize, and manage a journey together in real time. Plan My Stop should make group travel effortless by combining AI, live collaboration, shared decisions, and synchronized updates.

---

# Purpose

Planning a group trip is often difficult.

People have

- Different budgets
- Different interests
- Different schedules
- Different food preferences
- Different expectations

Plan My Stop should make group planning feel effortless.

---

# Vision

Instead of

One person planning everything,

Everyone contributes.

AI coordinates.

The experience feels like collaborating inside a shared workspace.

---
