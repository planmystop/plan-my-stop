# Premium Experience Guidelines

**Project:** Plan My Stop

**Version:** 1.0

**Status:** Approved

**Owner:** UX & Design Team

# Purpose

This document defines the premium experience standards for Plan My Stop.

Premium is not expensive.

Premium is not flashy.

Premium is the feeling users experience every time they interact with the product.

Every screen should communicate

Quality

↓

Craftsmanship

↓

Trust

↓

Comfort

↓

Speed

↓

Delight

---

# Vision

We are not building

a website.

We are building

the world's most premium AI-powered travel experience.

Users should immediately think

"This feels like an app."

rather than

"This looks like a website."

# First Impression

within

30 seconds.

Professional

↓

Minimal

↓

↓

# Visual Simplicity

Remove everything that

does not help the user.

Every component should justify its existence.

The best interface

contains less,

not more.

---

# White Space

Whitespace is luxury.

Whitespace creates

Focus

↓

Readability

↓

Comfort

↓

Confidence

↓

Premium Feel

Never fear empty space.

Crowded interfaces feel inexpensive.

---

# Layout

One Screen

↓

One Purpose

↓

One Goal

Avoid pages that attempt to solve multiple unrelated problems.

---

Support

Smooth scrolling

↓

Swipe gestures

↓

Bottom sheets

↓

Floating buttons

↓

Pull to refresh

↓

Context menus

↓

Soft animations

↓

Instant feedback

# Colors

Primary Background

White

Secondary Background

Very Light Gray

Cards

White

Borders

Soft Gray

Accent

Brand Color

Status

Semantic Colors

Color should communicate

meaning,

not decoration.

# Cards

Cards are the primary building block.

Every card should include

Title

↓

Primary Information

↓

Supporting Information

↓

Action

↓

Status

Cards should feel

touchable,

comfortable,

and lightweight.

---

# Shadows

Shadows should be

soft

↓

natural

↓

layered

↓

subtle

Never use heavy shadows.

Elevation should communicate hierarchy,

not decoration.

---

# Corner Radius

Cards

20px

Buttons

16px

Dialogs

24px

Images

24px

Bottom Sheets

28px

Inputs

16px

Rounded corners create

approachability.

---

# Typography

Typography should feel

Premium

↓

Readable

↓

Relaxed

↓

Balanced

Use

Large headings

↓

Comfortable body text

↓

Consistent spacing

↓

Minimal line length

Avoid dense paragraphs.

# Photography

Photography tells the story.

Images should be

Large

↓

Authentic

↓

Emotional

↓

Bright

↓

Natural

# Motion

What changed?

↓

Why?

↓

Where should I look?

↓

What happens next?

only for beauty.

# Motion Style

Animations should feel

Natural

↓

Responsive

↓

Smooth

↓

↓

Calm

200 ms

# Micro Interactions

↓

↓

↓

↓

# Loading Experience

Never show

blank loading screens.

Prefer

Skeletons

↓

Progress Indicators

↓

Meaningful Messages

Example

"Finding the best scenic route..."

instead of

"Loading..."

---

# AI Experience

AI should be

embedded,

not isolated.

Examples

Journey

↓

"Leave 20 minutes earlier."

Hotel

↓

"This matches your travel style."

Restaurant

↓

"Highly rated for families."

Map

↓

"Heavy traffic ahead."

The AI quietly improves the experience.

their destination,

not

# Forms

Forms should feel

like conversations.

Use

Smart Defaults

↓

Auto Fill

↓

Inline Validation

↓

Helpful Errors

↓

Progress Indicators

# Empty States

Empty states should inspire action.

Every empty state includes

Illustration

↓

Explanation

↓

Primary Action

↓

Helpful Tip

Never display empty screens.

# Success Moments
