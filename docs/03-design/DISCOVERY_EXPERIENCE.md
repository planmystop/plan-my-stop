# Discovery Experience

> Discovery is one of the core differentiators of Plan My Stop. Rather than helping users search for places, the platform helps users discover meaningful experiences tailored to their journey, preferences, context, and intent.

---

# Purpose

People don't travel to visit locations.

They travel to create experiences.

Plan My Stop should transform

Searching

↓

into

Discovering.

---

# Vision

Discovery should feel like

Having an expert local guide travelling with you.

The platform should continuously answer

"What amazing thing should I not miss?"

---

# Discovery Philosophy

Discovery should be

- Personalized
- Context Aware
- Explainable
- Timely
- Useful
- Delightful
- Local
- Intelligent

Users should never feel overwhelmed.
