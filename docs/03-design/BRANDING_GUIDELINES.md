# Branding Guidelines

> The Plan My Stop brand represents intelligent travel, trusted guidance, premium experiences, and memorable journeys. Every visual and verbal touchpoint should consistently reinforce these values across all products, platforms, and communications.

---

Branding is more than a logo.

It is the complete experience users remember.

Our brand should communicate

Trust

↓

Adventure

↓

Intelligence

↓

Reliability

↓

Premium Quality

Every interaction strengthens the brand.

# Logo Variants

↓

Horizontal Logo

↓

Stacked Logo

↓

Icon Only

↓

↓

Dark Background Version

Never create unofficial variants.

---

# Logo Clear Space

Maintain a minimum clear space
equal to the height of the logo icon.

No text or graphics should enter this area.

---

# Minimum Logo Size

Digital

24 px minimum height

Print

10 mm minimum height

Never scale below legibility.

---

# Logo Usage

Use

✓ Official files only

✓ Correct proportions

✓ High resolution

✓ Sufficient contrast

Never

✗ Stretch

✗ Rotate

✗ Add shadows

✗ Add outlines

✗ Change colors

✗ Distort proportions

# Accessibility

Branding must remain accessible.

Ensure

- Sufficient contrast
- Readable typography
- Recognizable icons
- Alternative text
- Keyboard accessibility

Accessibility strengthens the brand.

---

# Social Media Branding

Profile Images

Use official icon.

Cover Images

Feature journeys and landscapes.

Tone

Helpful

Inspirational

Professional

Never sensational.

---

# Marketing Materials

Maintain consistency across

- Website
- Mobile App
- Social Media
- Presentations
- Advertisements
- Email Campaigns
- Print Materials

Users should recognize the brand instantly.

---

# Co-Branding

When partnering with other organizations

- Maintain equal visual importance
- Preserve logo integrity
- Follow clear space rules
- Use official partner assets

Never compromise brand identity.

---

# Merchandise

Approved merchandise

- T-Shirts
- Caps
- Stickers
- Vehicle Decals
- Travel Kits
- Notebooks

Use official colors and logo only.

---

# App Store Assets

Maintain consistency for

- App Icon
- Screenshots
- Promotional Images
- Feature Graphics
- Preview Videos

The store presence should reflect the product quality.

---

# Brand Governance

Any branding change requires

- Design Review
- Product Review
- Accessibility Review
- Final Approval

Maintain one source of truth.

---

# Performance Considerations

Optimize

- SVG logos
- Responsive images
- Lazy loading
- Retina assets
- Vector illustrations

Brand assets should never reduce application performance.

---

# AI Implementation Notes

AI-generated interfaces must

- Use official colors
- Use approved typography
- Follow spacing tokens
- Preserve logo integrity
- Respect accessibility standards

Brand consistency applies to AI-generated screens.

---

- Premium Brand Variants
- Event Branding
- Partner Experiences

Core identity must remain unchanged.
