# Journey Timeline Design

> The Journey Timeline is the heart of the travel experience. It transforms a route into a complete travel story by organizing every moment before, during, and after the journey into an intelligent, interactive timeline.

---

# Purpose

Most travel applications answer

"How do I reach my destination?"

Plan My Stop answers

"What will my entire day look like?"

The Journey Timeline becomes the user's travel companion.

---

# Vision

The timeline should answer

- What is happening now?
- What happens next?
- How much time remains?
- Why is this stop important?
- What should I prepare for?

The user should understand the entire journey within seconds.

---

# Design Philosophy

The timeline is

Not

A schedule.

Not

A list.

It is

A living journey.

Every event should have meaning.

---
