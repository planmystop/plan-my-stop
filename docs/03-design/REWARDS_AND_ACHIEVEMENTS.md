# Rewards and Achievements

> Rewards and Achievements recognize meaningful travel experiences, celebrate milestones, and encourage exploration. They should create lasting satisfaction without encouraging unhealthy engagement or artificial usage.

---

# Purpose

Travel itself is the reward.

The platform simply recognizes it.

Achievements should celebrate

- Exploration
- Discovery
- Learning
- Family Moments
- Safe Travel
- Community Contributions

Never screen time.

---

# Vision

Users should feel proud

because of where they have been,

not because they opened the app.

Recognition should reflect real experiences.

---

# Philosophy

Travel

↓

Experience

↓

Achievement

↓

Recognition

↓

Memory

↓

Inspiration
