# AI Explainability

> AI recommendations are valuable only when users understand and trust them. This document defines how Plan My Stop explains AI decisions, recommendations, predictions, and confidence throughout the platform.

---

# Purpose

Artificial Intelligence should never behave like a black box.

Users deserve to understand

- Why something was recommended
- Why something changed
- Why something was predicted
- Why something was hidden
- Why something was prioritized

Trust is built through transparency.

---

# Vision

Users should never ask

"Why did AI recommend this?"

Because

The application already explains it.

---

# Philosophy

AI should

Explain

↓

Recommend

↓

Learn

↓

Improve

Never

Guess silently.

---

# Explainability Principles

Every AI decision should be

- Transparent
- Honest
- Understandable
- Actionable
- Context Aware
- Evidence Based
- Non Technical

# AI Should Explain

The AI should explain

- Route Selection
- Stop Recommendation
- Restaurant Ranking
- Hotel Ranking
- Budget Estimate
- Weather Prediction
- Journey Score
- Traffic Prediction
- Delay Prediction
- Safety Alerts

Everything important must have a reason.
