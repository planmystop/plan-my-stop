# Business Discovery Experience

> Business Discovery is the intelligent engine that connects travelers with businesses that genuinely improve their journey. Instead of displaying nearby businesses, Plan My Stop recommends the right business at the right moment based on context, preferences, timing, and journey intelligence.

---

# Purpose

Business Discovery should solve one question.

"What business would genuinely improve my journey right now?"

The answer should always be personalized.

---

# Vision

Traditional Maps

↓

Show nearby businesses.

Plan My Stop

↓

Quality is more important than quantity.
