# Partner Portal

> The Partner Portal is the centralized platform that enables businesses to manage their presence, analyze customer engagement, promote services, and interact with the Plan My Stop ecosystem.

---

# Purpose

The Partner Portal provides businesses with self-service tools to manage their listings while giving Plan My Stop a scalable business ecosystem.

The portal should reduce manual administration and allow verified businesses to manage their own information.

---

# Objectives

The Partner Portal should

- Allow businesses to register
- Manage business listings
- Update business information
- Manage images
- Respond to customer reviews
- View analytics
- Purchase premium services
- Track customer engagement

# Business Permissions

Partners can

- Edit their own listings
- Upload images
- Respond to reviews
- Manage promotions
- View analytics

Partners cannot

- Access other businesses
- Modify platform rankings
- Remove customer reviews
