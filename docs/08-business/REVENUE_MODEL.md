# Revenue Model

> This document defines how Plan My Stop generates revenue while maintaining an excellent user experience. Every monetization strategy should create value for users before generating revenue.

---

# Purpose

The Revenue Model defines

- Revenue streams
- Pricing strategy
- Business growth
- Customer value
- Future monetization

Revenue should always be the result of solving user problems.

---

# Business Philosophy

Our philosophy is simple.

> Help users first.

> Build trust.

> Generate revenue naturally.

Users should never feel forced to pay.

Premium features should enhance the experience rather than restrict essential functionality.

---

# Objectives

The revenue model should

- Be sustainable
- Be scalable
- Be transparent
- Support global expansion
- Support multiple revenue streams
- Avoid excessive advertisements

---

# Revenue Streams

The platform supports multiple revenue sources.

---

# 1. Premium Subscription

Users can subscribe to unlock premium features.

Examples

- Unlimited AI Journey Planning
- Advanced Route Optimization
- AI Travel Assistant
- Offline Trips
- Premium Maps
- Smart Notifications
- Multi-Day Itinerary Planning
- Trip Sharing
- Unlimited Saved Trips
- AI Travel History

Future

Monthly

Yearly

Family Plan

---

# 2. Business Listings

Businesses can pay for enhanced visibility.

Examples

- Restaurants
- Hotels
- Fuel Stations
- EV Charging Stations
- Attractions
- Cafes

Business Features

- Verified Badge
- Featured Listing
- Promotional Banner
- Analytics Dashboard
- Customer Insights

---

# 3. Sponsored Recommendations

Businesses may sponsor recommendations.

Sponsored recommendations

- Must always be clearly labelled.
- Must never replace organic recommendations.
- Must always follow quality standards.

User trust is always the highest priority.

---

# 4. Affiliate Revenue

Revenue generated from partner platforms.

Examples

- Hotel Booking
- Flight Booking
- Car Rental
- Travel Insurance
- Activity Booking

The user completes the booking through the partner platform.

Plan My Stop receives an affiliate commission.

---

# 5. Booking Commission

Future capability.

Bookings made directly inside Plan My Stop.

Examples

- Hotels
- Tours
- Activities
- Parking
- Camping
- EV Charging Reservations

Revenue

Commission per booking.

# 9. Advertising

Advertising should be limited.

Allowed

- Travel Promotions

Not Allowed

- Misleading Ads
