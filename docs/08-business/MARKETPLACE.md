# Marketplace

> The Marketplace is the business ecosystem of Plan My Stop where travelers discover trusted businesses and businesses connect with potential customers through intelligent recommendations.

---

# Purpose

The Marketplace connects users with verified businesses while maintaining recommendation quality, transparency, and user trust.

The Marketplace is not simply a business directory.

It is an intelligent recommendation ecosystem.

---

# Objectives

The Marketplace should

- Connect users with businesses
- Increase business visibility
- Generate business revenue
- Improve traveler experience
- Maintain recommendation quality
- Scale globally

---

# Recommendation Policy

Businesses cannot buy higher rankings.

Ranking is determined by

- Relevance
- User Preferences
- Journey Context
- Distance
- Ratings
- Reviews
- Quality

Sponsored listings must always be clearly identified.

---

# Reviews

Users may

- Rate Businesses
- Write Reviews
- Upload Photos
- Report Abuse

Businesses may

- Respond to Reviews
- Report Fake Reviews
