# 1. Product Definition

Plan My Stop is an AI-powered Journey Intelligence Platform that helps travelers discover, plan, optimize, and experience better road trips.

Unlike navigation applications, Plan My Stop focuses on the complete journey rather than only reaching the destination.

The platform combines AI, route intelligence, personalization, local discovery, business partnerships, and travel insights into one unified experience.

---

# 2. Product Vision

To become the world's most trusted platform for intelligent road travel.

Whenever someone thinks about a road trip, Plan My Stop should be the first application they open.

---
